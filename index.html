IGDTUW presents
Innerve

The tech-fest of IGDTUW is back with a banger.

Innerve, the annual technical fest of Indira Gandhi
Delhi Technical University for Women (IGDTUW),
started in 2015 and all these years it has been
witnessing phenomenal gathering. Innerve has
an overall reach of 100 thousand plus students
in Delhi and NCR. Leaded by a student force of
200 passionate girls.It aims to become a platform
for students to test their engineering skills in
different fields and unleash their horses. The sole
vision of Innerve is to boll down theories into
applications and shape a platform where students
could interact and lay down stronger ties.
